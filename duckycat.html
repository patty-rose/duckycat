My cat Ducky

origin of Ducky cat

Duckycat came into my life in 2012 as a pet left behind by a roomate. His meow sounded like a quack, so he became "Duck". And eventually everyone called him "Ducky cat". After promising my roomate I would find him a new home, and then failing, I realized I loved him and we were a family.

Reasons to love Duckycat

  • He's so fluffy!
  • He's very sweet :)
  • He's very lovable.
